About O-SMS

This web application was conceptualised by engineering department of commissioning crew - INS Kochi (Y-12702). Subsequently, a window based software O-SMS (beta version) was developed by in-house efforts and it served as backbone of the ship for the future commissions of the ship. This upgraded version of O-SMS will surely serve a long way in assisting ship staff in efficient management of onboard spares(OBS).
